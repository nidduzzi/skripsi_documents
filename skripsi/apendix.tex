For \(\Omega_{i,j}=K(x_i,x_j)=\exp\left(-\frac{\norm*{x_i-x_j}^2}{2\sigma^2}\right)\)

For example, with \(\sigma=1\), \(x_i=0.0\), \& \(x_j = 0.33\dots \)
\begin{equation}
  \begin{split}
    K(0.0,0.33) & = \exp\left(-\frac{\norm*{0.0-0.33}^2}{2(1)^2}\right) \\
                & = \exp\left(-\frac{0.33^2}{2}\right)                  \\
                & = 0.9460
  \end{split}
\end{equation}
